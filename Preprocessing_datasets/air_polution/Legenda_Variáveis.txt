Timestamp: Date and time of observation. 

Date_observed: Date of observation. 

Time_observed: Time of observation. 

Latitude: Geographic latitude of the observation location. 

Longitude: Geographic longitude of the observation location. 

NO2: Nitrogen dioxide concentration. 

NOx: Nitrogen oxides concentration. 

O3: Ozone concentration. 

Ox: Combined oxidants (ozone and NO2) concentration. 

PM1: Particulate matter <1 micron concentration. 

PM10: Particulate matter <10 microns concentration. 

PM25: Particulate matter <2.5 microns concentration. 

CO: Carbon monoxide concentration. 

Local_name: Name of the observation location.
